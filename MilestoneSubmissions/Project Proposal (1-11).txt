4. Application Name: CU Function Finder (CUFF)

5. Application Description: Our application is a CU Party finder, where students can create/host events as well as RSVP/sign up for other student’s events. We’ll prompt users to create an account which will be integrated into a “friends” system where you can request and add other CU Boulder students as friends on our platform. Our software will also have calendaring functionality so that the logistics for these parties (such as entry fee, theme, time, and location) can be formalized and easy to sign up for and set up.

For the events themselves we’ll have the option for the event to be marked as public (available to all CU Boulder students who have signed up for our service) or private events (events that are only viewable/available to friends or specified users). We plan on having the publicly listed events appear on the homepage for our web application. We’ll also have a ratings system where you can provide users with a “guest” or “host” rating, which will allow users to provide feedback about other users and will incentivize users to be good guests and good hosts. Lastly, we’ll strive to integrate a message board and messaging chat rooms so that our users can communicate with each other directly on our platform.

6. Audience: Our target audience would be CU Boulder students. Our software is meant to create a means for CU Boulder students to find and host parties and events.

7. Vision Statement: To offer a feature-rich, streamlined party planning and finding experience for students of CU Boulder.
